# Payroll Snapshot Strategy

# CSRM Payroll System

---

# PURPOSE

Defines:
historical payroll preservation strategy.

---

# IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE

Payroll should preserve:
historical business truth.

---

# SNAPSHOT AREAS

Payroll stores snapshots of:

- employee info
- salary structure
- payment info
- organization info

---

# WHY SNAPSHOT IS REQUIRED

Future changes must NOT modify:
old payroll history.

Examples:

- department transfer
- salary increment
- bank account change
- designation change

---

# IMPORTANT RULE

Payroll history should remain:
historically accurate forever.

---

# FUTURE FEATURES

- snapshot diff viewer
- payroll comparison
- historical analytics
