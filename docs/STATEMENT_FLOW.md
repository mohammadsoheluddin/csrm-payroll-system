# Statement Flow

# CSRM Payroll System

---

# PURPOSE

Statement acts as:
Payroll Accounting Distribution Layer.

It distributes:

- salary
- deductions
- bank amount
- cash amount

---

# INPUT SOURCES

- Salary Sheet
- Time Bill
- Bonus Sheet

---

# MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES

## Deduction Processing

- suspense
- AIT
- loan
- advance
- other deduction

---

## Payment Distribution

- bank payable
- cash payable

---

# IMPORTANT

One employee may receive:

- full bank payment
- full cash payment
- partial bank + partial cash

Statement handles this split.

---

# OUTPUTS

- Salary Statement
- Cash Statement
- Bank Statement
- Accounting Summary

---

# FUTURE FEATURES

- payment approval
- accounting integration
- suspense tracking
- deduction audit
- statement export
