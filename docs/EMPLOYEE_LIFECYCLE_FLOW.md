# Employee Lifecycle Flow

# CSRM Payroll System

---

# PURPOSE

This document explains:
employee lifecycle management flow.

---

# EMPLOYEE FLOW

Recruitment
↓
Joining
↓
Probation
↓
Confirmation
↓
Movement
↓
Increment
↓
Promotion
↓
Transfer
↓
Resignation / Termination

---

# EMPLOYEE CREATION

Employee receives:

- employee ID
- department
- designation
- salary structure
- payment info

---

# IMPORTANT RULE

Employee ID:

- permanent
- unique
- non-editable
- never reusable

---

# MOVEMENT TYPES

- transfer
- promotion
- increment
- designation change
- department change

---

# MOVEMENT IMPACT

Movement may affect:

- salary structure
- department
- designation
- reporting
- payroll grouping

---

# IMPORTANT PRINCIPLE

Historical data should remain preserved.

Old payroll records should NOT change after movement.

---

# TERMINATION FLOW

Employee status changes:

- resigned
- terminated
- inactive

Future payroll generation must stop.
