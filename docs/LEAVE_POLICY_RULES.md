# IMPORTANT RULES

## Paid Leave

Does NOT reduce salary.

---

## Unpaid Leave

Reduces salary.

---

# REPLACEMENT LEAVE RULE

Employee must:

- work on holiday
- have holiday attendance
- apply replacement later

System must validate:

- attendance existence
- holiday existence
- duplicate prevention
