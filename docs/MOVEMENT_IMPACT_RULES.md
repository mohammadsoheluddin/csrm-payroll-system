# Movement Impact Rules

# CSRM Payroll System

---

# PURPOSE

Defines:

- employee movement impact
- salary impact
- department impact

---

# MOVEMENT TYPES

- transfer
- promotion
- increment
- designation change
- department change

---

# IMPORTANT RULE

Movement should preserve:
historical payroll integrity.

Old payroll should NOT change.

---

# INCREMENT IMPACT

Increment may affect:

- gross salary
- designation
- reporting
- payroll grouping

---

# TRANSFER IMPACT

Transfer may affect:

- department
- branch
- reporting structure

---

# FUTURE FEATURES

- movement approval
- effective-date calculation
- historical organization tracking
